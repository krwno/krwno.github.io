How to get there

The closest international airport is Roma Fiumicino. From there, we suggest two options:

  1. Taking the bus from the arrivals at Fiumicino to Giulianova central station. This is operated by Gaspari Bus. Call your hotel to arrange transport from the train station: there might not be many taxis available, if at all.
  2. Renting a car and driving on the highway A24 in the direction of Teramo.

Where to stay

The seaside town of Giulianova is close to the venue and easier to reach by bus or car. We suggest booking a room in one of the many hotels available in Giulianova.

We have block reservations until March 31st at three hotels in Giulianova. All agreed-upon prices include breakfast. Be sure to mention "Matrimonio Di Remigio" when booking your room.

On May 29th, there will be a shuttle bus from Giulianova to the venue and back.
